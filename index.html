Personal Data Science Portfolio Website
A fully responsive, interactive portfolio designed to showcase data science skills, certifications, internships, and achievements in a visually compelling and professional format.

Build a professional digital presence that stands out to recruiters and hiring managers in the data science, AI/ML, and analytics field — making skills and achievements immediately visible.
